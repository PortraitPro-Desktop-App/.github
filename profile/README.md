## 🚀 Overview
PortraitPro for Windows is a dedicated retouching application built around portraits. Instead of asking you to mask, dodge, and burn every image by hand, it uses AI to detect facial features and apply smoothing, relighting, makeup, and subtle feature adjustments that keep results looking believable. Whether you shoot headshots, weddings, or everyday portraits, PortraitPro helps you turn raw captures into polished images while preserving skin texture and natural tone. The result is faster editing without the plastic, over-processed look that often gives retouching away.

## ✨ Features
- **AI face and skin detection** — Automatically locates the face, eyes, lips, and skin so edits apply precisely where they belong.
- **Natural skin smoothing** — Reduces blemishes and evens skin tone while keeping pores and texture intact for a realistic finish.
- **Relighting and shaping** — Adjusts the direction and softness of light to sculpt the face and correct uneven studio or on-location lighting.
- **Makeup and feature refinement** — Applies digital makeup, brightens eyes and teeth, and fine-tunes features with slider-based control.
- **Body and Studio editions** — Choose PortraitPro Body for natural body reshaping, or the PortraitPro Studio edition for extra workflow flexibility.

## 🎯 Getting Started
1. Open a portrait photo directly in PortraitPro.
2. Confirm the automatically detected face outline and feature points, adjusting them if needed.
3. Choose a preset as a starting point, or begin with the sliders for skin, lighting, and makeup.
4. Fine-tune each control until the result looks natural, comparing against the original as you go.
5. Save or export your finished image in the format that suits your workflow.

## ❓ FAQ
**Is PortraitPro free?**
PortraitPro is a paid product, but a free trial is available so you can test the retouching tools before buying. There is no full free copy of the paid editions.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
PortraitPro runs on modern 64-bit versions of Windows, including Windows 10 and Windows 11. For the smoothest experience, keep your system updated to a currently supported release.

**How does PortraitPro compare to Portraiture?**
Portraiture is a plugin focused mainly on skin smoothing inside a host editor, while PortraitPro is a broader standalone application that handles skin, relighting, makeup, and feature refinement with AI. The PortraitPro Studio edition also adds plugin support for editors like Photoshop and Lightroom along with RAW handling, which is useful if you want an integrated pipeline.
